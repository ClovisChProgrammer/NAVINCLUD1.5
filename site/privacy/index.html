Privacy Policy

NAVINCLUD — Chrome Extension for Color Accessibility

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Summary

NAVINCLUD respects your privacy. No personal data is collected, stored on servers, or shared with third parties.

Data Stored

The extension stores exclusively on your device (chrome.storage.local):

  • Filter preferences — filter type, intensity, and shift adjustment.

No test results are stored after the test assistant is closed.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ✕ Personally identifiable information (name, email, ID, address)
  • ✕ Browsing history or visited URLs
  • ✕ Credentials (passwords, tokens, cookies)
  • ✕ Analytics or tracking data
  • ✕ Any biometric or health data

Storage

All data is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local — the browser's internal, isolated storage area, not accessible to third parties. No data is transmitted to remote servers.

Third-Party Sharing

NAVINCLUD does not share any data with third parties.

Your Rights (LGPD)

In compliance with Brazilian Law No. 13.709/2018:

  • Confirmation and access: view your preferences in the extension popup
  • Correction: adjust filters manually via the extension controls
  • Deletion: remove the extension to erase all local data

Security

The extension uses Manifest V3 security model with the minimum required permissions. All processing is done locally in the user's browser.
