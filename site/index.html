Features

Everything you need for a more inclusive web experience.

🔬

Color Perception Test (Ishihara Method)

Interactive test with 18 Ishihara plates to help identify color vision types. Instant results with automatic filter recommendation.

🎨

8 Correction Filters

Dynamic filters for protanopia, deuteranopia, tritanopia, achromatopsia and their variations, with adjustable intensity and color shift.

📊

Monitor Calibration

Built-in monitor calibration guide with D65 standard, Gamma 2.2 and visual tests for maximum color accuracy.

🌐

Real-Time Filters

Filters are applied instantly to any website you visit, adapting colors on the fly for a seamless browsing experience.

🧩

Automatic Recommendation

Based on your Ishihara test results, the extension automatically suggests and applies the most suitable filter for your needs.

🔒

100% Local & Private

All data processed and stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to external servers. Your privacy is fully protected.
