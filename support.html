Frequently Asked Questions

Does NAVINCLUD work on any website?

Yes. Filters are applied to any web page. The content script runs on all URLs and the filter is applied directly to the HTML via SVG feColorMatrix.

How do I take the color blindness test?

Install the extension, click the NAVINCLUD icon on the toolbar, and select "INICIAR TESTE" (Start Test). Follow the on-screen instructions: 18 plates with 15 seconds each. At the end, you will receive a detailed diagnosis.

Do I need to calibrate my monitor?

We recommend calibrating before the test to ensure accuracy. Click "CALIBRAR MONITOR" (Calibrate Monitor) in the popup and follow the guide with D65 (6500K), Gamma 2.2 adjustments and visual tests.

Is my data sent to any server?

No. All test data and preferences are stored exclusively in your browser's chrome.storage.local. Nothing is sent to external servers. See our privacy policy.

How do I export my results?

In the extension's popup, click "EXPORTAR TODOS OS TESTES" (Export All Tests). The 3-step confirmation flow ensures you are aware of the extraction. Data is saved to your Downloads folder as a JSON file.

Is NAVINCLUD free?

Yes, completely free and open-source under the MIT license. No paid plans, subscriptions, or ads.

Does the test replace an eye exam?

No. The test has educational and awareness purposes. For clinical diagnosis, consult an ophthalmologist.

Quick Start Guide

1. Installation

Add the extension from the Chrome Web Store (link coming soon). Once installed, the NAVINCLUD icon will appear on the toolbar.

2. Calibration

Open the popup, click "CALIBRAR MONITOR" and follow the instructions to adjust color temperature, gamma and brightness.

3. Test

Click "INICIAR TESTE". Answer all 18 Ishihara plates within the time limit. At the end, see your detailed diagnosis.

4. Filter

With the diagnosis, select the corresponding filter in the popup. Adjust intensity and shift as needed. The filter applies instantly to all pages.
