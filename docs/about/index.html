About NAVINCLUD

Making the web more accessible for everyone.

What is NAVINCLUD?

NAVINCLUD is a Chrome extension that helps people with color vision deficiencies (CVD) navigate the web more comfortably. It combines a color perception test based on the Ishihara method with real-time color correction filters.

Whether you have protanopia, deuteranopia, tritanopia, achromatopsia, or any of their variations, NAVINCLUD can help make colors more distinguishable on any website you visit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NAVINCLUD a medical diagnostic tool?

No. NAVINCLUD is an interactive tool for color perception exploration. It does not replace ophthalmological exams or professional medical diagnosis.

Does the extension collect my data?

No. All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Which types of color blindness are supported?

Is the extension free?

Yes, NAVINCLUD is completely free and open source under the MIT License.

Can I adjust the filter intensity?

Yes. The popup includes sliders for intensity and color shift adjustment, plus an on/off toggle to quickly compare filtered vs unfiltered view.
